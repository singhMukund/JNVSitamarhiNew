About Us

Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya , Sitamarhi is an ideal institutes of sitamarhi which is situated at khairvi , 09 Kms away from sitamarhi falling Near Ramnagara Bazaar at Sonbarsha Road . In accordance with National Policy of Education(1986)govt. of India Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya started Presently the JNVS are spread in 29 state and 6 union territories. These are Co-educational residential schools fully financed by Govt of India. Students are selected through Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Selection Test (JNVST) in class VI and Through Lateral exam for class IX (as per availability of seats due to drop outs) . Since 2005-06 students are admitted in class XI in particular category on drop out seats on the basis of marks obtained in Matriculation . This vidyalaya is functioning in sitamarhi from 1994. The medium of instructions in JNV Sitamarhi is the mother tongue or regional language upto class VIII and English there after for Maths and science and or English for Social Science . Students of JNV Sitamarhi appear for X and XII examination from the Central board of Secondary Education

Mision

  • To promote national integration among students through a policy of migration
  • To encourage &promote talent predominantly from rural areas &weaker section
  • To provide quality modern education for all round rural development of talented children
  • To establish pace setting institution to be models in the district & to be resource center for promotion of excellence
  • To Prepare good citizen of India
