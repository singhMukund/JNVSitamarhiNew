Features:-

  • Good quality education for talented children pre dominantly from rural areas.
  • Candidates - Rural at least 75% , Urban utmost 25%
  • Reservation of seats for girls - 33%
  • Reservation of seats for Students from SC/ST categories in proportion to their population subject to the national minimum
  • Co educational and fully residential From class VI to class XII
  • Location - Rural areas . On the basis of offer of cost free land and rent free temporary building by state/UT governments
  • Free education including boarding and lodging as well as expenses on uniforms , text book stationery etc. But Rs. 200/- per month will be taken as general fee from the students of class IX to XII except SC,ST ,Girls , Physically Handicaped and all the students whose guardians are below the poverty line
  • Affiliated to CBSE
  • Admission in class VI through objective type test designed by CBSE and conducted by concerned district
  • Implementation of three language formula
  • Regional languages as medium of instruction for first three years .Hindi / English afterwards
  • 30% of students in class IX from a vidyalaya located in one linguistic area spend one academic session in a vidyalaya in a different linguistic area to promote national integration through understanding of the diversity and plurality of country's cultures and people
